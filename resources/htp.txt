Hector is in the mood for a quesadilla. But he doesn't have any cheese! Help Hector find cheese and drop it off at his home. But watch out! The local cat is loose! Avoid the hairballs, claws and the cat itself. They will hurt Hector. Scurry fast and collect as much cheese as you can.

Use the arrow keys to move, press escape to ESC to exit the game.
